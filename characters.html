The Characters

Overview

The entire cast features a 14-character (16 if you include the two proudcers) all-female group of musicians who were originally designed by Noriaki Kubo and are owned by VIZ Media. The primary line-up features 9 characters of 3 different phases for seven different instruments. The image on the left features the original line-up of Krowned Kueens.
